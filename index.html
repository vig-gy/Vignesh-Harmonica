About Vignesh

Award-winning harmonica artist with 17+ years of performance and teaching experience

🎵
Vignesh Chandrasekaran
Professional Harmonica Artist
🎶
📷 @vig_gy

Vignesh Chandrasekaran represents the new generation of harmonica artistry in Singapore. With over seventeen years of dedicated musical cultivation beginning in primary school, he has evolved from a curious student into an internationally recognized performer.

His recent achievements include securing 2nd Runners' Up at the prestigious Asia Pacific Harmonica Festival 2022 and earning Top 80 placement among global competitors at the Hong Kong International Chromatic Harmonica Competition 2022.

Recent Achievements

  • 2nd Runners' Up Award - Asia Pacific Harmonica Festival 2022
  • Top 80 Contestant - Hong Kong International Chromatic Harmonica Competition 2022

Musical Specialties

  • Movie themes
  • International folk music
  • Chromatic harmonica techniques
