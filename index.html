About Vignesh

Award-winning harmonica artist with 17+ years of performance and teaching experience

Professional Photo

With over 17 years of dedicated musical excellence, Vignesh Chandrasekaran has established himself as Singapore's premier harmonica artist and instructor. His journey began with a passion for musical expression and has evolved into international recognition, culminating in his achievement as 2nd Runners' Up at the Asia Pacific Harmonica Festival 2022.

Recent Achievements

  • 2nd Runners' Up Award - Asia Pacific Harmonica Festival 2022
  • Top 80 Contestant - Hong Kong International Chromatic Harmonica Competition 2022

Musical Specialties

  • Movie theme arrangements
  • International folk music
  • Chromatic harmonica techniques
