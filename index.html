About Vignesh

The journey behind the music

Vignesh Chandrasekaran picked up the harmonica over 17 years ago and never put it down. What began as a fascination with a pocket-sized instrument has grown into a lifelong pursuit of musical expression, taking him from local stages in Singapore to international competition podiums across Asia.

The harmonica taught me that the smallest instruments can hold the biggest stories.
