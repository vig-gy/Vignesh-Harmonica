Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need my own harmonica to start?

+

Yes, you'll need a harmonica for lessons and practice. I'll recommend the right instrument based on your chosen course and budget during our consultation. Basic harmonicas start from around $30-50.

How long are the lessons?

+

Standard lessons are 60 minutes, which provides adequate time for instruction, practice, and feedback. 30-minute sessions may be available for younger students or as intro sessions.

What if I'm not sure which course is right for me?

+

That's perfectly fine! Select "Free Consultation" in the course type, and we'll discuss your goals, experience, and preferences to recommend the best program for you.

Are online lessons as effective as in-person?

+

Yes! With proper audio setup and video quality, online lessons can be very effective. Many students prefer the convenience, and I've developed specialized techniques for remote harmonica instruction.

Do you offer HAS examination preparation?

+

Yes, HAS (Harmonica Association of Singapore) exam preparation is integrated into the Beginner Fundamentals (Grades 1-3) and Intermediate Mastery (Grades 4-5) courses.

What's your cancellation policy?

+

I understand schedules can change. Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations to reschedule without penalty. Details will be discussed during booking.
